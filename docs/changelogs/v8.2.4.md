## Why Apache-2.0?

The Apache License 2.0 provides:

- **Patent Protection**: Explicit grant of patent rights from contributors
- **Contribution Terms**: Clear terms for contributions
- **Trademark Protection**: Protects project trademarks
- **Compatibility**: Compatible with many other open source licenses

---

## What This Means for Users

- **No breaking changes** to the API or functionality
- **Same freedoms**: You can still use, modify, and distribute OfX freely
- **Patent grant**: Contributors grant patent rights, providing additional legal protection
- **Attribution**: Include the license and copyright notice when redistributing
